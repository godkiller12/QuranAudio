About Us

Welcome to Quran Audio Hub, your source for high-quality Quran recitations.

Our mission is to make the words of Allah easily accessible to everyone around the world. We feature a curated collection of renowned reciters in various riwayat.

This project is inspired by the wonderful service provided by QuranicAudio.com and aims to provide a similar experience for users who wish to host their audio locally.

All audio files are sourced by the user and are expected to be stored locally for this website to function.

The global audio player at the top allows you to continue listening as you navigate through different pages of the site, providing a seamless audio experience.
